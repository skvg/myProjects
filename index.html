The Role of AI in Board Games
This review paper shows the role of AI in board games. Board games have long been seen as an ideal test-bed for the study of AI. This paper shows how AI can be used in the games. It can be used as a opponent player and dummy players. AI can be used to build new balanced board games for the game designers.
